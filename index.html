About Me

My professional journey includes: guiding cross-functional Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning development teams and specialized Data Science teams, facilitating complex AI/ML model implementation for the banking sector; facilitating processes and improvements in the teams using Scrum, Kanban, Nexus and SAFe framework; being a part of one the biggest Business Intelligence solution in the insurance industry history.

Before dedicating myself to the world of Agile, I served nearly a decade as a Merchant Navy Mechanical Engineer Officer. This unique maritime background has equipped me with extensive operational experience and strong problem-solving capabilities in diagnosing and resolving complex technical issues under pressure, often with limited resources. This foundation instilled an unwavering adaptability in the face of challenges.
